Welcome to The SMAaCKS Resort

Established in 1992, the SMAaCKS Resort was awarded The Panel's Most Popular Choice Award in the year 2018 and 2019. With an area of 1000 acres, the property holds 70 One King Size Bedrooms, 70 Deluxe Bedrooms and 40 Executive Studio Rooms. We also entertain catering on large events like weddings,academic and business confreneces. Bookings for catering need to made by taking an appointment only. The SMAaCKS is a 4-star hotel business located in the heart of Hamilton, New Zealand. This hotel was founded by three young businesspeople who wanted to excel in the tourism business. The SMAaCKS provides cozy and well-appointed rooms with a beautiful view of Hamilton city and the Waikato river. We also have a restaurant within the hotel with the top chefs in New Zealand, preparing the most memorable and savory food available for all our guests. Our aim is to provide exceptional customer service for all our guests, we have numerous activities and events within the hotel for guests of all ages to enjoy.
